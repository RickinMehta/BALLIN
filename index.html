B(ALL-IN) stores your goals, streaks, and All-In Score locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not transmit or store this data on any server. No account creation is required and we do not collect personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Data

Your chat messages are processed in real-time by Anthropic's Claude API solely to provide coaching responses. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any third-party purposes. Conversation history exists only in your current browser session and is cleared when you close the tab.

Cookies & Storage

We use browser localStorage to remember your goals, streak, and Pro status across visits. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics scripts. You can clear all stored data at any time by clearing your browser's site data.
